TECHMART ELECTRONICS - REFUND AND RETURN POLICY

RETURN WINDOW
- Standard products: 30 days from date of purchase
- Electronics and accessories: 15 days from date of purchase
- Software and digital products: Non-refundable once downloaded

ELIGIBILITY
- Item must be in original condition with all accessories and packaging
- Proof of purchase required (order number or receipt)
- Items damaged by misuse are not eligible for refund

REFUND PROCESS
1. Submit a return request at returns.techmart.com or call 1-800-TECHMART
2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 24 hours
3. Ship the item back using the prepaid label provided
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

REFUND METHODS
- Original payment method: 5-7 business days
- Store credit: Immediate upon return approval
- Exchange: Processed within 3-5 business days

EXCEPTIONS
- Opened software cannot be returned
- Items marked "Final Sale" are non-returnable
- International orders: Customer responsible for return shipping costs

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
- Report within 48 hours of delivery
- We will provide free return shipping and full refund or replacement
- Email photos of damage to returns@techmart.com
