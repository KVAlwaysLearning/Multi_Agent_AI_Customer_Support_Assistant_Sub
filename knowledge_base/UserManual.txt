ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Creating an Account:
1. Visit techmart.com and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
3. Verify your email address via the confirmation link
4. Complete your profile with name and phone number
5. Enable two-factor authentication for security

Resetting Password:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your email for the reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Create a new password (minimum 8 characters, 1 uppercase, 1 number)
5. Log in with your new password

Account Locked:
- Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
- Or contact support@techmart.com for immediate unlock

SUBSCRIPTION MANAGEMENT

Upgrading Your Plan:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Subscription
3. Select your desired plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade - takes effect immediately

Cancelling Subscription:
1. Go to Settings > Subscription > Cancel Plan
2. Confirm cancellation
3. Access continues until end of billing period
4. Data retained for 30 days after cancellation

CLOUD STORAGE

Accessing Cloud Storage:
- Visit cloud.techmart.com or use the TechMart app
- Upload files by dragging and dropping
- Share files by right-clicking and selecting "Share"
- Storage resets monthly for Basic plan

SUPPORT AND FEEDBACK

Submitting a Support Ticket:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Help > Submit Ticket
3. Describe your issue in detail
4. Attach screenshots if relevant
5. Expect response within 24 hours (4 hours for Premium)

Providing Feedback:
- Rate your support experience after each interaction
- Submit product feedback at feedback.techmart.com
- Join our beta program at beta.techmart.com
