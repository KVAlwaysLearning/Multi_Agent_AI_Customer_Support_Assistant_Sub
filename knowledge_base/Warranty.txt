TECHMART ELECTRONICS - WARRANTY POLICY

STANDARD WARRANTY COVERAGE
- Laptops and Computers: 1 year parts and labor
- Smartphones and Tablets: 1 year parts and labor
- Accessories (cables, chargers): 6 months
- Smart Home Devices: 2 years
- Monitors and Displays: 3 years

WHAT IS COVERED
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware failures under normal use
- Battery defects (capacity below 80% within warranty period)
- Screen defects (dead pixels exceeding 5 pixels)

WHAT IS NOT COVERED
- Physical damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Damage from unauthorized repairs
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, dents)
- Damage from power surges (use a surge protector)
- Water damage unless device is rated waterproof

TECHMART PREMIUM WARRANTY (OPTIONAL)
- Extends coverage to 3 years on all products
- Covers accidental damage (1 claim per year)
- Priority support with 4-hour response time
- Price: $49.99/year for devices under $500, $99.99/year for devices $500+

HOW TO CLAIM WARRANTY
1. Contact support@techmart.com with your order number and issue description
2. Our team will diagnose the issue within 24 hours
3. Ship device to our repair center (free prepaid label provided)
4. Repair or replacement completed within 7-10 business days

WARRANTY REGISTRATION
Register your product at warranty.techmart.com within 30 days of purchase for extended benefits.
